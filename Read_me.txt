This package is Global Map version 1 (national & regional) in user friendly format.
Data were produced by National Mapping Organization of respective countries and regions under their responsibilities and derived into other format by the Secretariat of ISCGM.
Metadata can be downloaded with the data in official format.

National Mapping Organization of respective countries and regions have the copyright of the data.
Please read the following sentence about use of this data and follow the instruction.

Belize: The global Map of Belize is free of charge for any purpose related to the Global Mapping project only.
However, if you intent to use the Belize's data for commercial purpose, you will need to get permission/approval from the Commissioner of Land and Surveys, Lands and Surveys Department,Ministry of Natural Resourves and Environment before using the dataset(s). You also need to show the credit "Global Map of Belize copyright ISCGM, and our logos on your final product. Please email us your contact information, particular data set that you will use and purpose of use.
Notice:Raster data set is under preparation.
